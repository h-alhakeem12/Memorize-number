Game Rules

-Several numbered blocks will appear on the screen for 3 seconds (using CSS).

-The player must memorize their positions before they disappear.

-After they disappear, the player must tap the blocks in numerical order.

-If the player makes one wrong move, they lose one life.

-The player has 3 lives total.

-When all lives are lost, the game ends and Restart button appear .
